Taqueeria was founded in 2018 by two CIS majors at Baruch College. Their goal is to bring Mexico to you from the traditional Mexican decor to the delicious home-made tacos full of authentic Mexican seasonings. Don't miss out on this experience, especially since it's in your own backyard!

Briant Illescas
As a praciticing Mexican, there's nothing more that Briant likes than some delicious tacos. Born in Cuernavaca, Morelos, Briant has a lifetime experience with authentic Mexican cuisine.
Luisa Lacayo
 As a faithful and practicing foodie, Luisa loves all kinds of food. However, Mexican cuisine has always been in her top 3 favorites. Born and raised in the southern area of the Bronx, Luisa is no stranger to Mexican customs and tradition. Now she has teamed up with Briant to bring it to you!
